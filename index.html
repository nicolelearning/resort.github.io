FIJI RESORT

Fiji, a country in the South Pacific, is an archipelago of more than 300 islands.

It's famed for rugged landscapes, palm-lined beaches and coral reefs with clear lagoons.

Its major islands, Viti Levu and Vanua Levu, contain most of the population.

Viti Levu is home to the capital, Suva, a port city with British colonial architecture.

The Fiji Museum, in the Victorian-era Thurston Gardens, has ethnographic exhibits.
